A Nigerian tech company that deals with digital communications

Founded in 2002, Iykcas Communications is a leading Nigerian tech company that has soared to great heights in revolutionizing digital communication technology. With a deep commitment to innovation and connectivity, Iykcas has become a cornerstone in Nigeria's tech landscape, transforming how individuals and businesses interact in the digital age.

Our Mission

To deliver seamless, innovative digital communication solutions that empower growth, enhance connectivity, and bridge the technological divide in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to national prominence, Iykcas Communications has consistently contributed to Nigeria’s digital transformation. With groundbreaking tools, tailored solutions, and an unyielding passion for excellence, the company has impacted lives, communities, and industries across the nation.

Founder: Ikechukwu Peter

The soul of Iykcas Communications is its visionary founder, Ikechukwu Peter, a proud son of Ada Agu Obollo Etiti, in Udenu Local Government Area of Enugu State, Nigeria. Born on 1st October 1964, Ikechukwu embodies the spirit of innovation and leadership. His birth on Nigeria’s Independence Day foreshadowed a life of impactful contributions to his nation’s growth.

From his roots in Enugu State, Ikechukwu Peter’s passion for communication and technology propelled him to build Iykcas Communications, a company that reflects his dedication to bridging gaps and creating opportunities. His unwavering vision has not only elevated his company but also enriched Nigeria's digital communication space.
